Communicate the significance: Provide an overview of the importance of the housing element in shaping the town's character and meeting the needs of its residents.

Satisfy comp plan requirements: Include an affordable housing program that meets the requirements of the Comprehensive Housing Production and Planning Act.

Meet local housing needs: Address the primary land use category affecting town residents and character, emphasizing the development and availability of housing that qualifies as low or moderate income.

Promote affordable development: Provide policy and zoning recommendations to support the development of affordable and diverse housing options, including strategies for achieving the statutory goal of providing 10% affordable housing units.

Incorporate green strategies: Consider elements related to energy efficiency, sustainability, and climate change, in accordance with state guidelines.

Collaborate with the community: The housing element should be developed in consultation with the community, addressing demographic data and the needs of the residents while ensuring consistency with state regulations and guidelines.

## **Measuring need from three groups' perspectives**

- 1. **Today's cost-burdened residents:** The portion of the current population that is paying more than 30% of their income on housing in Barrington.
- 2. **Tomorrow's new residents:** The households expected to move to Barrington over the next decade.
- 3. **Today's workforce:** The people who currently work within Barrington but might not be able to afford to live in town.
