## **NOTICE OF DEMOLITION PROCEDURES**

## **See Ordinance No. 2009-5 (attached) for specific details**

- 1. The applicant shall apply for a Demolition Permit by completing the application form via the Town's e-permitting site, OpenGov. The applicant submits:
  - a) Notice that a building or structure is going to be demolished or removed.
  - b) "Week of" date that the demolition is scheduled for.
  - c) \$100 for the Demolition Notice Fee (deposited to the Building Permit account).
  - d) Any required documentation for the Building Official (discontinued utility confirmations, certificate of insurance, etc.).
- 2. Once required documentation is received, the Building Office sends, via first-class mail, a notice to each abutter at least 10 days prior to the date of issuance of the demolition permit.
- 3. The applicant must post a sign at the site of the building or structure to be demolished for at least a 3-day period immediately prior to the date of demolition. The Building Office will provide this notice digitally via the permit portal.
- 4. The permit fee is \$6.00 per \$1,000 with a minimum fee of \$30.00. There is no CE/ADA fee charged. This fee is deposited to the Building Permit account.
- 5. The applicant must also provide to the Building Office a Demolition Bond Check OR a Surety Bond from an insurance company in an amount equal to the cost of the demolition project, which will be returned to the applicant once the job has been completed. The form for a Demolition Bond Check must be completed by the Building Official's office, and signed by Finance, the Building Official and Applicant (or person that provided the Bond Check) once the Bond Check is returned at the Finance Dept. If a Surety Bond, applicant must upload a copy to the attachments section of the permit portal to be included with the permit record.
