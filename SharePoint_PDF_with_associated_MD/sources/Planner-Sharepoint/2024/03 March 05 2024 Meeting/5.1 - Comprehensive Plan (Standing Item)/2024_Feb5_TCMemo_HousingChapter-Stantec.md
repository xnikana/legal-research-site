## Town of Barrington

PLANNING, BUILDING AND RESILIENCE DEPARTMENT Barrington Town Hall | 283 County Road | Barrington, RI 02806

## **MEMORANDUM**

TO: Philip Hervey, Town Manager

FROM: Teresa Crean, AICP – Director of Planning, Building and Resilience

DATE: February 1, 2024 (For 2/5/24 Council Meeting)

SUBJECT: Housing Chapter of Comprehensive Plan, \$15,000 municipal contribution

**Purpose**: Requesting approval for \$15,000 municipal contribution to supplement a RI Housing Municipal Technical Assistance Program (MTAP) grant award to complete the Housing Chapter of the Comprehensive Plan Update. The MTAP funding will cover \$100,000 of the Town's \$115,000 request to contract with Stantec Consulting Services, Inc, a RI Housing-approved consultant. The \$15,000 Town contribution can be supported from the Town's Comprehensive Plan/Zoning capital reserve account.

RI Housing has approved the Town's request for \$100,000 from the Municipal Technical Assistance Program (MTAP) for updating the Housing Element of the Town's Comprehensive Plan. MTAP is a program funded through the Housing Production Fund established by the RI General Assembly. The grant Program is administered by RI Housing, who pre-selected consultants to assist municipalities.

State approval of Barrington's 2015 Comprehensive Community Plan expires in 2025. The Town has set aside capital funding over the years to pay for the Plan update, with work to start this year. The MTAP funding is an excellent opportunity to complete the Housing chapter as it must reflect new state legislation effective January 1, 2024 that removes barriers to housing development and work towards compliance with the State's 10% affordable housing goal.

The MTAP funding will cover \$100,000 of the Town's \$115,000 request. The \$15,000 Town contribution will cover the inclusion of an infrastructure capacity analysis in the scope of work with Stantec.

Three of the MTAP's program's target areas will be addressed in the Housing Chapter update (Source: [https://www.rihousing.com/mtap/\)](https://www.rihousing.com/mtap/). Stantec will complete the following tasks:

Task 1. Housing Needs Assessment

Task 2. Initial Infrastructure Assessment

Task 3. Public Engagement

Task 4. Development of Housing Element and Action Plan

The final deliverable will be a highly graphic Housing Element component of the Town of Barrington's upcoming Comprehensive Plan that complies with the requirements of the Rhode Island Comprehensive Planning and Land Use Regulation Act (§45-22.2 RIGL) and any other applicable state and local requirements. In accordance with §45-22.2 RIGL, including goals and policies that further the goal of § 45- 22.2-3(c)(3).
