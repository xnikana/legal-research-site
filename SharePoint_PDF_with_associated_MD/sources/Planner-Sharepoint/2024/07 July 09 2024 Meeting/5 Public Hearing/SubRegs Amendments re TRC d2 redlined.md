## **§ 200-7 Technical Review Committee.**

A. There is hereby established a committee appointed by the Planning Board, to be known as the "Technical Review Committee (TRC)," which is established in accordance with R.I.G.L. § 45-23-56, to conduct technical reviews of all applications subject to the jurisdiction delegated under this chapter and Chapter 185, Town of Barrington Zoning Ordinance. The TRC shall meet on the last Tuesday of each month as needed, or as determined by the chairperson. All minutes of the TRC shall be in writing and shall be kept as permanent documentation of Committee proceedings.

B. The TRC shall consist of four (4) members. The administrative officer shall serve as chairperson. Additional members shall consist of the following: the Director of the Department of Public Works (or designee); the Chief of the Fire Department (or designee; the Town's Planning/Zoning Technician; a representative from the Town's on-call engineering and/or architectural consultants shall serve in an advisory capacity as needed.

C. The Planning Board shall adopt written bylaws for the conduct of TRC meetings. Participation of three (3) members of the TRC shall constitute a quorum of the TRC. No action shall be taken unless by the concurring vote of at least a majority of the members participating on a matter.

- D. The TRC shall provide guidance to an applicant with regard to procedure, technical requirements, and overall project design.
- E. Review of applications in an advisory capacity.
- (1) The application types listed below shall be reviewed by the TRC in an advisory capacity with a recommendation to the appropriate permitting authority.
- (a) Preapplication meetings for applications subject to these regulations;
- (b) Administrative subdivisions, administrative development plan review or minor modifications or changes to land development projects, subdivisions, or development plan review at the request of the Administrative Officer;
- (c) Minor land development projects and subdivisions subject to a public hearing;
- (d) Major land development projects and subdivisions, provided that the TRC reviews the application prior to the Planning Board's first meeting on the application;
- (e) Comprehensive permit pursuant to § 200-73 when applicable;
- (f) Development plan review applications as set forth in § 185-154; and
- (g) Other matters referred to the TRC by the Administrative Officer, Planning Board, Zoning Board or Town Council for advisory review.
- (2) For all such applications the TRC shall conduct its review in accordance with the procedures and standards set forth in these regulations and the Zoning Ordinance. When an application has been referred for recommendation to the TRC by a public body, the TRC shall forward its comments and recommendations, along with all other essential project information, at least seven days prior to the public body's first meeting at which the application is scheduled. ## **§ 200-7.1 Design Review Committee.**

A. There is hereby established a committee appointed by the Planning Board, to be known as the "Design Review Committee" which is established, to conduct technical reviews of all applications subject to the jurisdiction delegated under this chapter and Chapter 185: Zoning Ordinance. The Design Review Committee shall meet on the second Tuesday of each month as needed, or as determined by the chairperson. All minutes of the Design Review Committee shall be in writing and shall be kept as permanent documentation of Committee proceedings.

B. The Design Review Committee shall consist of four (4) members. The volunteer members, who may also serve on the Planning Board, should be persons who are registered or professionally trained in architecture, engineering, landscape architecture, historic preservation, or an allied field.

C. The Design Review Committee may adopt written bylaws for the conduct of Design Review Committee meetings. In the event the Design Review Committee does not adopt such bylaws, its meetings shall be governed by the most recent edition of Robert's Rules of Parliamentary Procedure. Participation of three (3) members of the Design Review Committee shall constitute a quorum of the Committee. No action shall be taken unless by the concurring vote of at least a majority of the members participating on a matter.

D. The Design Review Committee shall provide guidance to an applicant with regard to project design standards as set forth in this chapter and Chapter 185: Zoning Ordinance.

E. Review of applications in an advisory capacity.

(1) The application types listed below shall be reviewed by the Design Review Committee in an advisory capacity with a recommendation to the appropriate permitting authority.

(a) Sign permit review pursuant to § 185-94: where a sign application requires a variance and/or special use permit;

(b) Major land development projects and subdivisions, at the discretion of the Administrative Officer.
