Re: Application fee for Preliminary Site Plan Approval

- Since acquiring the property in 2011, Shineharmony has paid over \$1.5 million in property taxes to the town of Barrington without burdening the town's expenditures on education and municipal services.

  costs and interest rates, the financial burden on developers seeking to deliver such\nunits is very significant.
