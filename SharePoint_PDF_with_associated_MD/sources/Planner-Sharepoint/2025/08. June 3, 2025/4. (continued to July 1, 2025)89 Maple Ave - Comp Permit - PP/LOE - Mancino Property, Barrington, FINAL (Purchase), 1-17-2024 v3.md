## **Letter of Eligibility: Mancino Property, 89 Maple Ave, Plat 23, Lot 058, Barrington**

Dear Mr. Mancino:

We have received your application for a letter of eligibility to pursue a Comprehensive Permit in the town of Barrington. You have represented that you propose a two-unit affordable development. The affordable units will be sold to households earning a maximum of 80% of the Area Median Income ("AMI").

- 1. One unit will have two bedrooms, two bathrooms, and a half bathroom.
- 2. One unit will have three bedrooms and one bathroom.
  - a. We highly recommend for the 3-bedroom unit, the Owner consider adding a one-half bathroom (toilet and vanity) in the final architectural plans.

Based on the current AMI, the Freddie Mac Primary Mortgage Market Survey 30-year fixed-rate mortgage average interest rate as of the date of this letter, anticipated taxes, insurance and mortgage insurance costs, the maximum sales price in Barrington for an affordable Condominium unit priced for households earning 80% of the AMI, is \$198,289.00 for a two-bedroom unit, and \$220,558.00 for a three-bedroom unit. The maximum permitted sales price may change in the future due to changes in any of the above noted inputs.

In addition to requirements for affordability and monitoring, under R.I.G.L. 45-53, the construction or rehabilitation of qualifying low-and-moderate-income housing units must be assisted and remain affordable by a state, federal, or municipal subsidy program that will be documented in a long-term land lease and/or deed restriction recorded with the town of Barrington.

After preliminary review of the plans and financial assumptions for the proposed sale two (2) affordable condominium units to households earning 80% of the AMI, all to be located at Plat 23, Lot 053 in Barrington, RIHousing has determined that:

- This project appears to be eligible for assistance under Article XXVII Low- and Moderate-Income Housing: Mandatory Inclusionary Zoning Requirements, 185-191 - Purpose, of the Town of Barrington.
- In conformance with R.I.G.L. 42-55-5.3 RIHousing performed an on-site inspection of the site and has reviewed pertinent information supplied by the applicant.

Mancino Property January 17, 2024

- The initial project proforma was reviewed and the project appears to be feasible based on projected overall development costs and potential sources.
- The applicant, Mancino Property, has provided evidence of site control.

Based upon the review of the submitted information and supporting materials and based on the representations in your request, **Mancino Property is eligible to pursue a Comprehensive Permit application in the town of Barrington to develop Plat 23, Lot 058.**

Modifications to the development proposal resulting from local review do not require a revised letter of eligibility.

Thank you for your commitment to providing affordable housing opportunities to low- and moderate-income Rhode Island families.

Sincerely,

Anne Berman Director of Real Estate Development 1/18/2024
