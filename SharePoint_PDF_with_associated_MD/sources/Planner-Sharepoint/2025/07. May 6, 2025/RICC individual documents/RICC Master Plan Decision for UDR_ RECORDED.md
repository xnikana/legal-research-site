MOTION: Member Larry Bacher seconded by Vice Chair Rua, moved to conditionally approve the Special Use permit for the Unified Development Review (UDR) submitted at 150 Nayatt Road conditioned on obtaining Final Plan approval of the UDR (Major Land Development). No dimensional variance is requested or approved.

In conditionally approving this application for a special use permit, the Planning Board finds:

- 1. The use is authorized under §185-8C in the RE district.
- 2. With compliance with Master Plan conditions of approval it will be in general harmony with the purposes of chapter 185 as expressed in §183-3, especially purposes A, B, C, D, E, F, G, K, L and M.
- 3. The applicant meets the criteria of §185-73.1J by providing a list of chemicals applied to the grounds.

MOTION: Member Larry Bacher seconded by Member Kilmartin, moved to approve the Master Plan for the Unified Development Review (Major Land Development Project) submittal at 150 Nayatt Road with the following conditions:

- 1. Maintain or replace existing street lighting at the property entrance to Nayatt Road.
- 2. Sidewalks to comply with §200-45.1 Standards for sidewalks.
- 3. Storm drainage system to comply with \$200-49 Storm Drainage Systems incorporating Best Management Practices. Specifically include oil separators as required by \$200-49E
- 4. Provide a plan of the proposed sanitary sewer and the connection with details for approval by DPW for the Preliminary plan submittal.

- 5. Sanitary sewer connections shall be provided with cleanouts and backflow preventers to the satisfaction of DPW.
- 6. Secure a sewer permit from DPW. Comply with DPW requirements for inspection of all work.
- 7. Provide an erosion and sediment control plan as required by §200-50
- 8. Buildings design to comply with §200-51.1A Building Standards particularly in regard to paragraphs (2), (5), (7), (8) and (9).
- 9. Electric transformer to be screened in compliance with §200-51.1A(9)
- 10. Landscaping to comply with §200-51.1C(2) Landscaping
- 11. Provide protection for street trees on Nayatt Road adjacent to the current paddle ball courts (snow fencing or equivalent) and indicate this on the Preliminary submission.
- 12. Consider subsurface tree survival infrastructure (Permavoid or similar) for tree plantings on parking islands. Provide revised detailed specifications for the Preliminary submission.
- 13. Lighting to comply with §200-51.1C(2)(g) including "Dark Sky" guidelines
- 14. Submit a revised parking plan to comply with \$200-51.1C(2)(c) Parking Areas and with \$200-51.1C(2)(k), (l), (m) and (n)
- 15. Show storage areas on the revised plan for Preliminary submission. Service area south of the proposed Cart Barn to be screened to comply with §200-51.1C(2)(d).
- 16. All improvements are to be constructed in compliance with §200-52 Required Improvements
- 17. Fire Department to approve building layout.
- 18. Review with staff applicability of 200-45.2 Bicycle Standards.

## In reaching this decision, the Planning Board finds:

- 1. The proposed development is consistent with the comprehensive community plan;
- 2. The proposed development is in compliance with the standards and provisions of the municipality's zoning ordinance, with the zoning relief granted through unified development review;
- 3. There will be no significant negative environmental impacts from the proposed development as shown on the final plan, with all required conditions for approval; and
- 4. The proposed land development has adequate and permanent physical access to a public street.
