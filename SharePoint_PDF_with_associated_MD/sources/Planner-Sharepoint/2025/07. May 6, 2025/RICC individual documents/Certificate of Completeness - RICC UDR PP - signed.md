Subject to verbal agreement made by the applicant's engineer on March 21, 2025 with the Administrative Officer the following information requires submittal as stated:

- 1. Submittal of a <u>narrative</u> including:
  - a. Statement that the application's detailed specifications satisfy best management practices for "oil separators". If not, written statement that any Final Plan application submittal will include a set of revised plans and detailed specifications to ensure the oil separator requirement is met.
  - b. Evidence that collaboration with the Fire Department has occurred regarding the department's approval of the building layout.
  - c. Agreement that a Class I survey for the "southeast corner" of the RICC property will be prepared and submitted as part of any Final Plan application submittal.
  - d. An indication and/or affidavit that a RIDEM wetlands permit is not required since it's wrapped in with the currently pending CRMC application.
