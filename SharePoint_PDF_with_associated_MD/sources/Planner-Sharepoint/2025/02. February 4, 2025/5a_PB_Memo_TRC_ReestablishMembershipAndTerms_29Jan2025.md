## **MEMORANDUM**

TO: Barrington Planning Board

CC: Amy Goins, Assistant Town Solicitor

FROM: Herbert A. Durfee, III DATE: January 29, 2025

SUBJECT: Technical Review Committee – Reestablishment, Members, & Terms

Over the last couple of legislative sessions, the Rhode Island General Assembly amended the law pertaining to the Technical Review Committee (TRC). While there is likely some amendment necessary to the committee's written rules of procedure, that matter can be considered for future discussion.

For now, though, it's recommended that the Planning Board "re-establish" the TRC's membership and respective terms.

## Suggested motion:

*It is moved, pursuant to RIGL § 45-23-56, that the Barrington Technical Review Committee (TRC) is re-established as a 7-member advisory committee comprised of the following members and terms:*

|   | Member:                                                   | Term Ending:              |
|---|-----------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------|
| • | Administrative Officer and Chair, Herbert A. Durfee, III, | Permanent; no term<br>end |
| • | Full Member<br>and Vice-Chair, Jon Staback                | 10/31/2026                |
| • | Full Member, Larry Bacher                                 | 10/31/2026                |
| • | Full Member, Jeff Bardell                                 | 10/31/2027                |
| • | Full Member, Richard Godfrey                              | 10/31/2027                |
| • | Full Member, Ed Wetherill                                 | 10/31/2028                |
| • | Full Member, Jordan Durham                                | 10/31/2028                |

*Terms of Full Members are for three years. To protect the staggering of terms, any vacancy of a Full Member position shall be filled by appointment of the Planning Board for the balance of the respective term period remaining.*

*It is further moved to request the Administrative Officer, in collaboration with the Assistant Town Solicitor, that updated written rules of procedure be prepared for the Planning Board's discussion and adoption before the end of fiscal year FY 2024-25 but preferably sooner.*
