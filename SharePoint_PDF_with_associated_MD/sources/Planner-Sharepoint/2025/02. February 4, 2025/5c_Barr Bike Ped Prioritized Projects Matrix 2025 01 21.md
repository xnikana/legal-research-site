## **Barrington Bicycle/Pedestrian Implmentation Plan Prioritized Projects for Implementation**

**DRAFT - 1/21/2025**

*Note 1: This list is composed of key roadway segment improvements that, once completed) will fill existing "gaps" in the existing bicycle and pedestrian facility network in Barrington RI. Filling those gaps results in a townside, interconnected network that users feel safe to utilize, thuse reducing short vehicle trips.* 

*Note 2: Projects in BOLD are in the process of concept design development by Fuss & O'Neill for completion in February 2025.* 

*Notes 3 Ranking method: Projects were scored using the following inputs, and then all results tallied and weighted.*

- *July 2024 BPAC Workshop Results: Network Gap Prioritization Vote Tallies*
- *Scaled results (scale 0-4) from July 2024 BPAC Workshop Results: Network Gap Prioritization Vote Tallies*
- *2022 Complete Streets Plan Survey Results (total votes by public)*
- *Scaled results (scale 0-4) of 2022 Complete Streets Plan GIS Mapping Survey Results (total votes by public)*
- *Importance to Overall Barrington Bike and Pedestrian Network Connectivity as rated by Fuss & O'Neill (Score 0-4)*
