Notice is hereby given that the Barrington Planning Board will hold a public hearing on Tuesday, January 7, 2025, at 7:00 p.m. on the following:

Discuss and Act: Unified Development Review – Master Plan. Rhode Island Country Club, Inc., 150 Nayatt Road, AP 06, Lot 001 (Parcel for Proposed Development) – Demolition of existing storage cart barn, accessory sheds, equipment building, and concrete pads. Proposing a new cart barn, tennis court, multi-use building and relocated paddle ball court. Resurface existing paved parking area and add additional spaces southwest of the cart barn. Installation of stormwater best management practices, utilities, and associated landscaping.

The hearing will be on the agenda of the Planning Board's regular business meeting to be held in the Council Chamber at Town Hall, 283 County Road. All interested parties may attend and be heard by the Board. Plans may be viewed at Town Hall in the Building and Planning Office in the Lower Level of Town Hall upon request, or online at www.barrington.ri.gov/planningboard.

The Town Hall is accessible to people with disabilities. Individuals requiring any accommodation for disabilities must notify the Barrington Town Clerk's office at 247-1900 Ext. 301 (Barrington Public Library TDD, 247-3750) in advance of the meeting date.
