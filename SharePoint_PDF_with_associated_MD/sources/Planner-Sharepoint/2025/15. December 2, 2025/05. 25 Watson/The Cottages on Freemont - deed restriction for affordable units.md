DEED RESTRICTION

This Deed Restriction is hereby granted on this \_\_\_ day of
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_, 202\_ ("Effective Date") and in favor of the
TOWN OF BARRINGTON, a municipal corporation, with a mailing address of
283 County Road, Barrington, Rhode Island 02806 ("Town") (as such deed
restriction may be amended from time to time, this "Deed Restriction").

> WITNESSETH:

A.  That certain real property located at \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_, in
    > Barrington, Rhode Island 02806; Assessor's Plat \_\_ Lot \_\_\_-\_
    > and as more fully described in [Exhibit A]{.underline} attached
    > hereto ("Property") was developed with subsidy provided by the
    > Town ("Funds'); and

B.  In contemplation of said subsidy and in recognition of the benefit
    > received therefrom, the undersigned hereby agrees to the recording
    > of this Deed Restriction memorializing the rights and obligations
    > of the undersigned in consideration of the Funds.

NOW THEREFORE, for good and valuable consideration, the receipt and
adequacy of which is acknowledged by the parties hereto, the parties
hereto hereby agree as follows:

1.  Affordability Period.

    a.  This Deed Restriction shall remain in effect for a period of
        > \_\_\_\_\_\_\_ (XX) years from the date of the certificate of
        > occupancy of the Property (the "Affordability Period").

    b.  During the Affordability Period, the Property shall continuously
        > be used as affordable housing such that the incomes of those
        > individuals owning the residential units at the Property is
        > restricted to \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ of the area median income
        > ("AMI") ("Affordability Restrictions").

2.  Recording and Filing; Covenants Run with the Property.

    a.  Upon execution and delivery hereof the Town shall cause this
        > Deed Restriction and all amendments hereto to be recorded and
        > filed in the official public land evidence records of the city
        > or town in which the Property is located.

    b.  The Affordability Restrictions shall (i) run with the
        > Property, (ii) encumber the Property, (iii) be binding upon
        > the undersigned and its heirs, transferees, successors and
        > assigns for the time period set forth below, and (iv) are not
        > merely a personal covenant of the undersigned. 

    c.  The undersigned hereby agrees that any and all requirements of
        > the laws of the State of Rhode Island reensure to be satisfied
        > in order for the provisions of the Affordability Restrictions
        > to become effective and constitute a deed restriction and
        > covenant running with the Property are deemed to be satisfied
        > in full, and that any requirements of privity of estate are
        > deemed satisfied or, in the alternative, that an equitable
        > servitude has been created to ensure that the Affordability
        > Restrictions run with the Property.

    d.  If the undersigned desires to sell, convey, or otherwise
        > transfer the Property, the undersigned shall promptly notify
        > the Town in writing of such intent ("Notice of Intent to
        > Transfer") by certified mail return receipt requested and any
        > such sale is subject to the terms of Section 4(g) below.

3.  Default.

    a.  In the event of any default or breach of the terms hereof by the
        > undersigned, or any permitted successor, such party (or
        > permitted successor) shall proceed immediately to cure or
        > remedy such default or breach. In case such action is not
        > taken or not diligently pursued, or the default or breach
        > shall not be cured or remedied within sixty (60) days of such
        > notice by the Town, a housing-related governmental or
        > regulatory agency, or such other entity vested with the
        > authority to enforce affordable housing covenants, of such
        > default and demand for cure by the Town or otherwise, the
        > undersigned, such noticing entity, or the Office of the
        > Attorney General of Rhode Island, or its designee, shall have
        > the authority to institute such proceedings as may be
        > necessary to cure and remedy such default or breach,
        > including, but not limited to, proceedings to compel specific
        > performance by the party in default or breach of its
        > obligations.

4.  Miscellaneous

    a.  [Successors and Assigns.]{.underline} This Deed Restriction
        > shall be binding upon the parties hereto and their respective
        > heirs, transferees, successors, and assigns.

    b.  [Governing Law]{.underline}. This Deed Restriction shall be
        > governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the
        > State of Rhode Island applicable to contracts made and
        > performed in such state.

    c.  [Severability]{.underline}. If any term or other provision of
        > this Deed Restriction are invalid, illegal, or incapable of
        > being enforced by any law or public policy, all other terms or
        > provisions hereof shall nevertheless remain in full force and
        > effect so long as the economic or legal substance of the
        > transactions contemplated hereby is not affected in any manner
        > materially adverse to any party. Upon such determination that
        > any term or other provision is invalid, illegal, or incapable
        > of being enforced, the parties shall modify this Deed
        > Restriction so as to effect the original intent of the parties
        > as closely as possible in order that the transactions
        > contemplated hereby are consummated as originally contemplated
        > to the greatest extent possible.

    d.  [Notices]{.underline}. All notices to be given pursuant to this
        > Deed Restriction shall be in writing and delivered by hand or
        > by any nationally recognized overnight delivery service sent
        > to the parties hereto at their last known address.

    e.  [Amendment.]{.underline} The undersigned agrees that it will
        > take all actions necessary to effect amendments to this Deed
        > Restriction as may be necessary to comply with any and all
        > applicable laws, rules, regulations, policies, procedures, or
        > court rulings.

    f.  [Maintenance of the Premises]{.underline}. The undersigned will
        > protect and maintain the Property or cause to be protected and
        > maintained in good order, repair and condition at all times,
        > subject to ordinary wear and tear.

    g.  [No Transfer of Ownership Interests]{.underline}. During the
        > Affordability Period, the undersigned shall not (i) sell,
        > convey, transfer or encumber all or any portion of the
        > Property; or (ii) transfer, assign or grant a security
        > interest in all or a portion of an entity owning the Property,
        > in each event unless: (I) prior notice is provided to
        > Town, (II) the transferee meets the Affordability
        > Restrictions, (III) the Town provides its prior consent in
        > writing, and (IV) if needed, a replacement Deed Restriction is
        > recorded in Land Evidence Records.
