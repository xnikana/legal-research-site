- topography is relatively steep. There is a large coastal marsh and beaches just south of the project property.
- B. The project site is located at 25 Watson Avenue in the Town of Barrington and is approximately 7.3 acres. The property is currently occupied by the former Carmelite Monastery which includes a large building, parking lot, concrete sidewalks, fences, retaining walls, and utility poles. The property also contains a small cottage building northwest of the monastery building. The Town is responsible for the demolition of the monastery site. The rest of the site is a combination of grass and woods and has relatively steep slopes. A portion of the property is within the 200-foot buffer of a coastal feature and therefore, is within the jurisdiction of the RI Coastal Resources Management Council (CRMC). The property is also within a Natural Heritage area. A very small portion of the property is within the FEMA 0.2% Annual Chance Flood Hazard floodplain. The property does not have any wetland resources on site. The proposed design includes subdividing the parcel into eight lots, including six single family lots, one multi-family lot with twelve cottages, and one open space lot.
