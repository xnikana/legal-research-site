October 3, 2025

## 25 Watson Avenue Preliminary Plan Review Application Supporting Materials.

The Applicant requests waivers for the below requirements as written in
Chapter 200 of the Barrington Code of Ordinances.

1.  **200-45.1 Standards for sidewalks: Sidewalks shall be at least 4 ½
    feet in width.**

    A.  The applicant is proposing 4-foot-wide sidewalks within Parcel
        #6 (multifamily development). This is appropriate given the
        expected use. Sidewalks will meet ADA requirements.

2.  **200-45.2 Bicycle parking standards: All multifamily developments
    shall provide lockable bike rack spaces equaling at least 20% of the
    total number of off-street parking spaces being provided.**

    A.  The applicant is not proposing bicycle racks as a part of the
        multifamily development (Parcel #6). This is appropriate for the
        cottage court residential development layout.

3.  **200-45.3 Fee in lieu of sidewalk installation or bicycle parking
    facilities.**

    A.  N/A -- The applicant is requested the fee in lieu of sidewalk
        installation or bicycle parking facilities to be waived.

4.  **200-47 Lots: Frontage, building coverage, minimum area, and
    setbacks.**

    A.  The applicant is requesting a waiver on land use. The proposed
        development is within a R40 district which is defined by a
        density of one dwelling unit per acre. The proposed development
        includes a 1.73 acre multi-family dwelling lot with 12 units.
        This is a density of 1 unit per 0.14 acres.

5.  **200-51.2 Curb cut standards: All curb cuts shall be limited to one
    per development.**

    A.  The applicant is proposing individual driveways for each
        single-family lot and two driveway entrances for the multifamily
        cottage court lot (Parcel #6).

6.  **200-51.3 Residential Site Standards: Buildings and site
    improvements shall minimize changes to the existing topography and
    loss of existing mature vegetation.**

    A.  The applicant is requesting a waiver for this requirement due to
        the inability to maintain existing vegetation due to the high
        density of units on the multifamily lot (Parcel #6).

7.  **200-52 B. Required Improvements. B. Curbs. All streets in new
    developments shall have granite curbing.**

    A.  The applicant is requesting a waiver for this requirement and is
        proposing cape cod bituminous berm as a substitution.
