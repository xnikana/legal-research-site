# **MINUTES**

### **Planning Board Capital Subcommittee**

### **9:30 a.m. Wednesday, February 13, 2025**

### **Council Chamber, Town Hall**

*Subcommittee Members Attending: Brian Rua, Larry Bacher, Heather Kilmartin, Roni Phipps (Alternate)*

*Attending from Town Administration: Town Manager Philip Hervey, DPW Superintendent John Renquinha, DPW Assistant Superintendent Carlos Pedraza, Fire Chief Gerald Bessette, Police Chief Michael Correia, Library Director Kris Chin, Library Technology Coordinator Patrick Elliot, Senior/Recreation Director Jean Bellm*

The meeting was called to order at 9:38 a.m. Brian Rua was unanimously approved (3-0) to serve as Chair of the Subcommittee. The Subcommittee reviewed the CIP Summary Spreadsheets and project request forms with representatives from Town Departments.

During discussion of proposed energy and other building projects at the Public Safety Building, Member Bacher recommended that the Town sign a maintenance contract with a vendor to ensure the systems are working correctly throughout the year. Manager Hervey said the Town will be looking at funding this in the operating budget. Chair Rua said the committee will need a cost estimate to complete larger projects at the Public Safety Building, including roof replacement. Member Bacher suggested exploring the use of Spencer Trust funding to purchase a new bus with wheelchair access for the Senior Center since it would benefit those in need and be used by Barrington residents. (Request totaled approximately \$180,000 over four years.)

The Committee reviewed each capital input from Departments in developing its recommended capital budget, which totaled \$2,610,299 in FY2026, and ranged between \$1.6 million to \$2.1 million in future years over the 6-year timeframe of the Capital Improvement Program (FY2027-FY2031).

**Member Bacher made a motion, seconded by Member Kilmartin, to forward to recommend approval of the FY2026 Capital Budget, as revised** (see Planning Board Capital Subcommittee Recommended FY2026 Capital Budget, attached). Motion was approved, 3-0.

On a motion by Member Bacher, Chair Rua adjourned the meeting at 11:30 a.m.
