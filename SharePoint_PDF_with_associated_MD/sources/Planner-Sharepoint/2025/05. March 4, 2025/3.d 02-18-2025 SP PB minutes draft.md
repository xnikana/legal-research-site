# **BARRINGTON PLANNING BOARD**

### **Special Business Meeting Minutes**

7:00 PM, Tuesday, February 18, 2025 Council Chamber – Barrington Town Hall 283 County Road, Barrington, RI

### **1. Call to Order.**

Chair Roni Phipps calls the meeting to order at 7:00pm.

### **2. Roll Call and Determination of Quorum.**

*Present:* Lawrence Bacher, Douglas Caito, Peter Costa, Connor O'Neal, Roni Phipps (Chair), Brian Rua (Vice Chair)

*Also Present:* Herb Durfee, Town Planner; Genevieve Elliot, Planning/Zoning Assistant

### **3. New Business**

## **3.1 Discuss and Act: Planning Board's Recommended Six-Year Capital Improvement Program (CIP), FY26.**

Town Planner Herb Durfee briefly described the annual process of the Planning Board Subcommittee meeting with the Town Manager and Department Heads about the CIP and making recommendations to the Town Council. Planner Durfee explained the goal of the Town Manager was to have the new money not exceed \$1.5 million and the recommendation of the subcommittee was \$1,515,556. Planner Durfee further expressed that what the Board is doing is taking action to recommend the capital budget, as they may decide to amend or not, to the Town Manager for further consideration.

Chair Phipps announced that Member Brian Rua was elected Chair of the subcommittee, and he would be giving the Board the report from that subcommittee meeting.

Vice Chair Rua mentioned that the original ask from the various departments was \$2.63 million. Vice Chair Rua explained that their recommendation of \$1,515,556 still managed to get most of the department asks into the budget.

Vice Chair Rua explained the major uses of the budget:

- ➢ Police Chief asked for another \$200,000 for ceiling tiles, was budgeted last year, so money will come from capital reserve account.
- ➢ Police and Public safety building roof work, budgeted at \$50,000 this year and for next few years. Using estimate and can amend accordingly.
- ➢ They removed a \$140,000 roadway item from the budget, noting that DPW has \$142,000 in their reserve account currently and they can use that this year for those expenses.
- ➢ Senior Bus wheelchair lift, they recommended the director of senior services speak with Spencer Trust for a grant for the bus.
- ➢ Vet's Memorial Park lights and installation for \$448,000 was moved to the bond page. Since the Town is going up for bond, will put this item their along with Chianese Baseball Dugout covers.

Vice Chair Rua explained that beyond those major items, only a few minor adjustments were made. He explained that since they are using funds from the reserve this year that they are decreasing what is being put in that reserve this year.

**MOTION: Member Bacher seconded by Member Caito moved to approve the Sub-committee'<sup>s</sup> recommendation on the CIP budget. Motion carried unanimously.**

### **4. Adjourn**

**MOTION: Member Bacher seconded by Vice Chair Rua made a motion to adjourn the meeting at 7:05pm. Motion carried unanimously.**
