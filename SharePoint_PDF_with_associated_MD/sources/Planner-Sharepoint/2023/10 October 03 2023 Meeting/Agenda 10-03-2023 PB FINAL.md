## **BARRINGTON PLANNING BOARD**

## **Regular Business Meeting**

7 p.m. Tuesday, October 3, 2023 Council Chamber – Town Hall 283 County Road, Barrington, RI

**Meeting materials can be found online at<https://www.barrington.ri.gov/182/Planning-Board>**

## **AGENDA**

- 1. Call to Order.
- 2. Roll Call and Determination of Quorum.
- 3. Consent Agenda
  - 3.1 Approve Minutes September 5, 2023 Regular Business Meeting
- 4. Old Business
  - 4.1 Discuss and Act: Comprehensive Permit Review, Preliminary Plan Submittal for 173R Maple Avenue, AP 23, Lot 325 in the Residence-10 and Neighborhood Business Zone. Applicant proposes creation of four units on the site: Unit 1 is a first-floor apartment that would result from renovation of the existing garage onsite to an accessible LMI unit (1,440 square feet); Unit 2 is the existing market rate unit, which will remain unchanged (1,440 square feet); Units 3 & 4 will be new market-rate dwelling units attached to the existing two-car garage to the south (1,064 square feet each). Master Plan approval for this application was granted in June 2021.
- 5. New Business
  - 5.1 Discuss and Act: Administrative Subdivision for 2 Seabrook Court, AP 32, Lot 25 in the R-25 District.
  - 5.2 Discuss and Act: Provide recommendation on sketches by Union Studio of the development at 25 Watson Avenue illustrating 14 cottages and 5 single-family lots (referred by the Town Council). [See page 3](https://barrington.ri.gov/DocumentCenter/View/1238/23_0824-CMC-FinalSiteScheme?bidId=) of the renderings provided to the Town on August 24, 2023, available at barrington.ri.gov/25watson.

**NOTE: Public comment will not be taken on item 5.2. Public comment is to be heard by the Town Council, who are the decision makers on the project at this time.** 

- 6. Other Business
  - 6.1 Discuss and Act: Provide recommendation on use table of the zoning ordinance for introduction to the Town Council at their November 6, 2023 Meeting.
