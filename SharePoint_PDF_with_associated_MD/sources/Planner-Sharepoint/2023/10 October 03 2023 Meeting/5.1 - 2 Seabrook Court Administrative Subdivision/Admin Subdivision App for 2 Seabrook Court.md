## EXHIBIT "A"

The proposed lot line shift to the west is limited to the last 8.64 feet of the north east corner of lot 25 owned by Jacqueline Jayne (Jayne) where it abuts the north west corner of lot 401 owned by Douglas and David Caito in their capacities as Trustees of the Seabrook Court Trust (Caito). Currently the eastern Jayne lot line common with Caito lot turns south east at the north west corner of the Caito lot until it intersects with Seabrook Court creating an odd hook to the north east corner of her lot.

The process of the proposed reconfiguration can be looked on as a North Eastward extension of the common lot line between the Jayne and Caito lots such that it intersects with directly with Seabrook Court eliminating the odd hook in the north east corner of the Jayne lot and making the Caito lot more appropriately even on Seabrook Court; or in the alternative the moving of 20.03 feet of the north west end of the Caito lot line to become even with Seabrook Court.

Either way this administrative subdivision, whether a shift or extension, will result in Jayne transferring 91 square feet of land to the Caito lot. This will result in the Jayne lot being 38,039 squared feet, the squaring of the Caito lot with Seabrook Court and the elimination of the odd northeastern hook in the Jayne lot.
