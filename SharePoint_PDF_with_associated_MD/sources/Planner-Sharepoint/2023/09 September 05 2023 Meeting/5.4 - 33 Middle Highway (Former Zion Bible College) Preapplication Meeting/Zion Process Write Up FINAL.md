## **Process for Major Land Development Project & Associated Amendments to Zoning Ordinance & Comprehensive Plan: 33 Middle Highway f/k/a Zion Bible Institute**

**Plat 14, Lots 2, 4, 13, 341, 342, & 343**

**September 5, 2023: Preapplication Meeting**

## **Timeline:**

*This timeline is based on assuming applicant wishes to submit as soon as possible. Timeline is subject to change.*

- 1) Master Plan Application (due Friday, September 29, 2023). *All are to be provided by the applicant/required for the application to be deemed complete.*
  - a. Requirements per checklist <https://barrington.ri.gov/182/Planning-Board>
  - b. Proposed Revised developer guidance/any additional Comprehensive Plan amendments.
  - c. Proposed zoning ordinance amendments.
- 2) In-person department review (between submission and October 12, 2023 Technical Review Committee Meeting).
- 3) Technical Review Committee Meeting (Thursday, October 12, 2023).
- 4) Certification of completeness/incompleteness within 25 days of submission. If incomplete, an additional 10 days after resubmission.
- 5) IF REQUIRED/NEEDED: Supplemental materials deadline for the Planning Board Informational Hearing (Tuesday, November 14, 2023).
- 6) Notice of the Public Information Hearing to be posted in the Barrington Times at least seven days in advance (Wednesday, November 22, 2023). **This deadline will need to be confirmed given Thanksgiving week.**
- 7) Planning Board Informational Hearing (Tuesday, December 5, 2023).
- 8) Notice required to the Barrington Times for three consecutive weeks prior to the Town Council public hearing.
- 9) Town Council Public Hearing on Revised Developer Guidance and proposed Zoning Ordinance/Comprehensive Plan amendments (Monday, February 5, 2024).
