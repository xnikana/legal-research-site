## **Urban Standards**

- 1. Regionally Influenced Architecture…An Authentic Architecture. Well proportioned simple forms with vertical openings, and simple roof lines.
- 2. Mix of housing types within each block… building types on blocks, at attract multi-generational buyers, and to help create a more resilient environment.
- 3. Small is Beautiful a mix of uses should include small cottages, senior homes and accessory dwelling units. Be contextual by keeping higher density towards the center of the site and lower density adjacent to existing SF neighborhoods.
- 4. Architecture that provides gifts to the street to promote walkability Provide courts, gardens, patios, stoops and porches. Hide the car.
- 5. Celebrate the site's history Use of stone along the base of buildings where appropriate.
