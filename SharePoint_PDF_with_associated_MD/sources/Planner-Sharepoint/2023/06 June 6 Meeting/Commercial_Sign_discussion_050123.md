## **AGENDA ITEM #28:**

Discuss and Act: Amending the Town's Commercial Sign Ordinance, Including a Potential Motion for Referral (K. Berard) TM Comment: An item included on the agenda at the request of Councilor Berard. Recommend referring specific issues / concerns related to the Town's commercial sign regulations to the Planning Board, Technical Review Committee and Economic Development Commission. Motion: No motion provided.

Councilor Berard put this forward after hearing a comment from a recent zoning meeting re: the Chipotle application It was mentioned that Barrington does not have any design standards, and should the Town consider design standards for signs in Town.

Councilor Kustell added that the EDC did take this up at one point where other signage ordinances and models were considered. The challenge is how do we communicate what we require. He also noted that several businesses have communicated their interest in more flexibility with signage regarding temporary signs for sales or other events, and signage to make sure residents and visitors know businesses are open in Barrington.

**Motion:** Refer the question of whether or not Barrington would like to see a unified design aesthetic in signage and/or create a design manual with photos for business partners in Town to Planning Board, TRC and Economic Development Commission. Motion passed unanimously.
