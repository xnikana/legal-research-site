# A N A C T

# RELATING TO TOWNS AND CITIES -- LOCAL PLANNING BOARD OR COMMISSION

\*\*\*

 This act would require initial, biennial, and continuing education in land use law and planning for local planning and zoning board members and members of historic district commissions. This act would take effect on July 1, 2022. ========
