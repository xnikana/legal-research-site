  - 3.1 Approve Minutes May 2, 2023 Regular Business Meeting
- 4. Public Hearing
  - 4.1 Discuss and Act: Preliminary Plan for Major Subdivision/Comprehensive Permit, 17 Lorraine Street

Major Subdivision/Comprehensive Permit Application: Robinson Property, 17 Lorraine Street. Proposal would subdivide a 15,687 square foot lot (Assessor's Plat 10, Lot 41) into three separate lots. The existing multi-family building will be demolished. The existing "old windmill" will remain; it will make up the single family residence on Lot #1. Lot #2 will have a single-family dwelling. Lot #3 will contain a two-family dwelling. The site is zoned Residence-10. One of the units, proposed as part of Lot #3, will qualify as Low-Moderate Income Housing. Relief from provisions of the Zoning Ordinance and Subdivision Regulations is being sought through the Comprehensive Permit process.

- 6. Old Business
  - 6.1 Discuss and Act: Training topics and schedule for summer Planning Board meetings
    - RI House Bill H7023 passed in June 2022, and requires initial, biennial, and continuing education in land use law and planning for local planning and zoning board members and members of historic district commissions. RI House Bill H5042 enacted in 2017 requires in (j) Each member of a planning board or commission shall participate in training and education classes concerning the effects of development in a flood plain and the effects of sea- level rise once every two (2) years. Each member shall complete two (2) hours of training in order to be certified for the two (2) years required by this subsection. Upon completion of the training, the planning board or commission member shall file with the municipal clerk a statement asserting that the training course has been completed.
