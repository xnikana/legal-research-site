## **Process for Major Land Development Project & Associated Amendments to Zoning Ordinance & Comprehensive Plan: 33 Middle Highway f/k/a Zion Bible Institute**

**Plat 14, Lots 2, 4, 13, 341, 342, & 343**

**DRAFT Process for Major Land Development Project (as of October 24, 2023). Please be advised that the process steps are subject to change with new legislation effective January 1, 2024:**

1) Master Plan Application

*All are to be provided by the applicant/required for the application to be deemed complete.*

- a. Requirements per checklist <https://barrington.ri.gov/182/Planning-Board>
- b. Proposed Revised developer guidance/any additional Comprehensive Plan amendments.
- c. Proposed zoning ordinance amendments.
- 2) In-person department review (between submission and Technical Review Committee Meeting).
- 3) Technical Review Committee Meeting.
- 4) Certification of completeness/incompleteness within 25 days of submission. If incomplete, an additional 10 days after resubmission.
- 5) IF REQUIRED/NEEDED: Supplemental materials deadline for the Planning Board Informational Hearing.
- 6) Notice of the Public Information Hearing to be posted in the Barrington Times at least seven days in advance.
- 7) Planning Board Informational Hearing.
- 8) Notice required to the Barrington Times for three consecutive weeks prior to the Town Council public hearing.
- 9) Town Council Public Hearing on Revised Developer Guidance and proposed Zoning Ordinance/Comprehensive Plan amendments.
