DATE: October 30, 2023

SUBJECT: Comprehensive Plan Chapter Reviews

The Town will be doing a full rewrite of the Town's Comprehensive Plan
to set the 20-year blueprint for the Town, as required by State law.
There are nine (9) elements/chapters to a Comprehensive Plan:

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **[Chapter of the Comprehensive     **[Board/Commission
  Plan]{.underline}**                 Assigned]{.underline}**
  ----------------------------------- -----------------------------------
  Housing & Neighborhoods             Housing Board of Trustees

  Economic Development                Economic Development Commission

  Circulation                         Bicycle-Pedestrian Advisory
                                      Committee

  Community Services & Facilities     Planning Board

  Natural & Cultural Resources        Conservation Commission

  Outdoor Recreation                  Park and Recreation (to be
                                      assigned)

  Energy                              Resilience and Energy Committee

  Natural Hazards                     Resilience and Energy Committee

  Land Use                            Planning Board
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

The Planning, Building, and Resilience Department is assigning a review
of each chapter of the Comprehensive Plan to relevant Boards &
Commissions while the Town secures a consultant for the Comprehensive
Plan rewrite. This review will provide the consultant team with
information they need to initiate the effort. With this request, staff
asks the Committee/individual members to provide feedback on the
chapter(s) assigned (see above). Responses are due by December 29, 2023.
