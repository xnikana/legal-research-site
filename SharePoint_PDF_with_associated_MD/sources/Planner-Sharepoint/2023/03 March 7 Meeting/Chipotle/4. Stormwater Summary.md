Regulatory Applicability

The project's stormwater system, detention, and treatment has been designed in accordance with the Town of Barrington's *Land Development and Subdivision Regulations* (Chapter 200), § 200-49(C) where: the runoff-rates were calculated for the design storm events up-to and including the 100-0year frequency rainfall event.

The project is within the Coastal Resources Management Council (CRMC) Wetlands Jurisdictional boundary, and is situated within a Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) designated floodplain Zone AE (100-Year). In concurrence with the Town of Barrington review process, GM2 will coordinate the project's approval with CRMC for a Freshwater Wetlands Permit in accordance with 650-RICR-20-00-9. The project will disturb greater than 10,000 square feet of existing impervious area, therefore GM2 will submit the project for Rhode Island Department of Environmental Management (RIDEM) review and obtain approvals as necessary.

Furthermore, as the proposed project includes improvements which will impact a State Highway drainage system (County Road), the Applicant will submit an application to the Rhode Island Department of Transportation (RIDOT) for a Physical Alteration Permit. Stormwater has been addressed in accordance with the RIDOT State Highway Right-of-Way Regulations (290-RICR-20-00-4).
