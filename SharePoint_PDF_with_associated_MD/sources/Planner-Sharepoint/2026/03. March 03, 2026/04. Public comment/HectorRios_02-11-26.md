## **Concerns Regarding Belton Court Village Development**

# **To the Barrington Zoning Board**

We, the residents and constituency of Barrington respectfully submit our concerns regarding the proposed Belton Court Village development. We are not against growth and Belton Court should be developed but **NOT** on the proposed scale. The submitted design is more appropriate for a larger urban setting with proper critical mass density. However, at the suburban level we believe this project is unsuitable for our town.
