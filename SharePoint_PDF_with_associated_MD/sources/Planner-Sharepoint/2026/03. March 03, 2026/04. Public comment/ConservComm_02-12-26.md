Roberts, Motion from the Barrington Conservation Commission and the Barrington Tree Commission regarding the proposal for the development of "Belton Court" presented in the Comprehensive Permit Request to request that the Barrington Planning Board meet all of the following regarding the development:
  - 1) require the protection of all existing trees designated on plans as to be protected with fencing or other barriers maintained at twice the size of the drip line of each tree (or collectively, as appropriate)
  - 2) maintain a minimum of 51% tree canopy coverage for the property in accordance with the Green Infrastructure Center Strategic Tree Canopy Plan commissioned and accepted by the Town of Barrington;
  - 3) require all plantings within the property to meet the designation of the Draft Native Planting Ordinance and/or meeting the standards as set forth in the Environmental Protection Agency Eco Region III, Northeast Coastal Zone [\(https://www.epa.gov/eco](https://gcc02.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epa.gov%2Feco-research%2Flevel-iii-and-iv-ecoregions-continental-united-states&data=05%7C02%7CGElliot%40barrington.ri.gov%7Ca67ea08775f849ffc95608de6a439085%7Cb30641b1e3da4c9583ff9dc2c19ca693%7C0%7C0%7C639065035599312911%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=%2FfhJGnwMvnCfkneNDB35d0DaM4OVDbvd5UxbIfhRVi8%3D&reserved=0)[research/level-iii-and-iv-ecoregions-continental-united-states](https://gcc02.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.epa.gov%2Feco-research%2Flevel-iii-and-iv-ecoregions-continental-united-states&data=05%7C02%7CGElliot%40barrington.ri.gov%7Ca67ea08775f849ffc95608de6a439085%7Cb30641b1e3da4c9583ff9dc2c19ca693%7C0%7C0%7C639065035599312911%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=%2FfhJGnwMvnCfkneNDB35d0DaM4OVDbvd5UxbIfhRVi8%3D&reserved=0));
  - 4) require all plantings to be mixed species avoiding monocultures within the property;
  - 5) require all roadside plantings to avoid blockage of viewsheds and limit traffic hinderance;
  - 6) require the full maintenance and preservation of Belton Court (a National Historic Registered property) and the landscaping within the Belton Court National Historic Registered District (an area of 4.5 acres) in accordance with the preservation of Natural and Cultural Resources in Chapter 5 of the 2015 Barrington Comprehensive Plan (or the updated plan, whichever is in force at the time of approval).
- D. Boyes, Second. Motion Approved 6-0-0-1.
