To the Planning Board,
According to the Barrington Assessor’s Database, a decision by the Barrington Building
Board of Review on 1/26/2021, and according to reporting in the Barrington Times
(“Vacant Property Owners in Barrington Must Secure Buildings,” 10/30/2020),
Shineharmony is not the sole owner of Belton Court (supporting documents enclosed).
This second owner is an individual and resident of Barrington, Mi Guangchun, who is
listed as co-owner of six parcels with ShineHarmony, including 33 Middle Highway. Their
connection to ShineHarmony and the project has not been disclosed in Planning Board
documents, the consultant’s presentations, or local reporting on the process to date.
It behooves the Planning Board to achieve satisfactory information about the second
owner and to share this information with all residents before proceeding. Residents
deserve to have a full and transparent accounting of all parties with a financial stake in
the outcome of this process.
My questions for the Planning Board and Town Attorney:
- Has due diligence been performed regarding the second owner to fully understand their
connection to the project-- including their motivation and resources-- which are
