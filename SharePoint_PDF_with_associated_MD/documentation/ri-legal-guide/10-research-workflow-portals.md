# RI Legal Research Guide — Section 10: Research Workflow, Portals, Tools, and Databases

Source: Rhode Island Public Records and Land Use Law: A Research Guide for Legal Practitioners
Prepared: March 2026

## 10. Research Workflow: Portals, Tools, and Databases

### 10.1 Starting a Land Use Research File

A systematic research file for a RI comprehensive permit proceeding should include records from the following six sources, in priority order:

1. **The planning board's official record** — obtain via APRA from the planning department; request all application materials, correspondence, peer review reports, staff memoranda, and meeting recordings
2. **The applicant's full submission** — often available via the municipality's SharePoint/CivicPlus document portal (linked from the town's official project page)
3. **The water authority's file** — APRA request for all correspondence with the developer and the municipality regarding the project
4. **The fire department's file** — APRA request for all correspondence, inspection reports, and any written comments on the project; request apparatus specification sheets for all responding apparatus
5. **RIDEM's file** — use RIDEM's online permit search: https://www.ri.gov/dem/wetlands/
6. **The Secretary of State's open meetings portal** — obtain all meeting minutes for all relevant boards: https://opengov.sos.ri.gov/openmeetings

### 10.2 Monitoring the Municipal Record in Real Time

Most RI municipalities publish hearing materials on a SharePoint or CivicPlus portal linked from the town's planning board page. These portals are typically updated 7–10 days before each hearing. Practitioners should:
- Bookmark the official town project URL
- Sign up for the municipality's CivicPlus notification system if available
- Monitor the Secretary of State's open meetings portal for new agendas referencing the project

### 10.3 Rhode Island Case Law Databases

**Free resources:**
- Justia RI Supreme Court: https://law.justia.com/cases/rhode-island/supreme-court/
- Justia RI Superior Court: https://law.justia.com/cases/rhode-island/superior-court/
- Google Scholar: Free RI Supreme Court opinions from 1950; search "Rhode Island" within "State courts"
- Cornell LII: https://www.law.cornell.edu/states/rhode_island
- RI Courts public portal: https://publicportal.courts.ri.gov/PublicPortal

**Subscription (comprehensive):**
- Westlaw — RI Supreme Court from 1828; Superior Court from 1991
- Fastcase — available to RI Bar Association members at no additional charge: https://www.ribar.com/
- Lexis — RI Supreme Court from 1828; Superior Court from 1980

Georgetown Law Library RI guide: https://guides.ll.georgetown.edu/c.php?g=277308

### 10.4 NFPA Standards Research

Complete NFPA standards library (free read-only access): https://www.nfpa.org/for-professionals/codes-and-standards/list-of-codes-and-standards/free-access

Key standards for multifamily development disputes:

| Standard | Topic | Free Access URL |
|----------|-------|----------------|
| NFPA 1 (2021) | Fire Code (RI adopted) | https://link.nfpa.org/all-publications/1/2021 |
| NFPA 13 (2022) | Sprinkler Systems | https://link.nfpa.org/all-publications/13/2022 |
| NFPA 101 (2021) | Life Safety Code | https://link.nfpa.org/all-publications/101/2021 |
| NFPA 1901 (2022) | Automotive Fire Apparatus | https://link.nfpa.org/all-publications/1901/2022 |
| NFPA 1142 | Water Supplies for Rural/Suburban Fire | https://link.nfpa.org/all-publications/1142/2021 |

NFPA LiNK requires a free account at nfpa.org. The viewer is read-only; text can be quoted verbatim. For citation purposes, note the document page number as displayed in the viewer.

### 10.5 Municipal Ordinance Research

Municipal zoning ordinances and subdivision regulations for RI communities: https://library.municode.com/RI

Note that Municode is periodically updated but may lag behind the most recently enacted amendments; verify currency against the town clerk's records.

### 10.6 Statewide Planning and GIS Resources

RI Division of Statewide Planning: https://planning.ri.gov/
RIGIS data clearinghouse (RI geographic data): https://www.rigis.org/
FEMA Flood Map Service Center: https://msc.fema.gov/portal/home
USDA Web Soil Survey (soil type, permeability, SHWT data): https://websoilsurvey.sc.egov.usda.gov/App/HomePage.htm
