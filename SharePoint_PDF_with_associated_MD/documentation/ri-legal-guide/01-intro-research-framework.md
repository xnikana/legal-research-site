# RI Legal Research Guide — Section 1: Introduction and Research Framework

Source: Rhode Island Public Records and Land Use Law: A Research Guide for Legal Practitioners
Prepared: March 2026 | Jurisdiction: Rhode Island

## 1. Introduction and Research Framework

Rhode Island's land use and public safety regulatory landscape sits at the intersection of state enabling legislation, adopted model codes, and municipal ordinances — each of which must be researched through distinct portals.

### 1.1 The Three-Layer Framework

**Layer 1 — State Statutes (RI General Laws)**
All statutes: https://webserver.rilegislature.gov/Statutes/
Free mirrors: Justia RI (https://law.justia.com/codes/rhode-island/), Cornell LII, FindLaw.

**Layer 2 — Rhode Island Code of Regulations (RICR)**
Agency regulations: https://rules.sos.ri.gov/organizations
RICR format: [Agency Title]-[Chapter]-[Subchapter]-[Part]. Example: RI Fire Safety Code is 450-RICR-00-00-7.
SOS open government center: https://www.sos.ri.gov/divisions/open-government-center/rules-and-regulations

**Layer 3 — Municipal Ordinances and Comprehensive Plans**
Municode RI: https://library.municode.com/RI
RI Division of Statewide Planning: https://planning.ri.gov/
